Taranza

From the Kespuzzuo Lore Compendium

Taranza is a character in the Kespuzzuo Fanwork Corpus, who made his first appearance in Skygardening. He is a spider with magic abilites, and is Sectonia's best friend.

As a character, he originates from the Kirby franchise.

Physical Appearance

Just like Sectonia, he is a cloud spider. This means that he is like a spider, but not like how a spider is usually depicted has. He floats in the air, and has 6 floating hands, but no legs. He has a silver bowl cut for his hair, and his skin can be described as brown, or "chestnut" colored.

He has two white eyes without pupils in his face, and two golden eyes with pupils above, through his hair. On the back of his head he has four golden eyes, just like the ones on front. His head has two yellow horns.

He is often seen wearing a green cloak, a red scarf, white gloves with orange parts, and a green shirt with buttons.
