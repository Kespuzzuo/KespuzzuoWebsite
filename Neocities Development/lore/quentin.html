Quentin

From the Kespuzzuo Lore Compendium

Quentin is a character in the Kespuzzuo Fanwork Corpus.

Physical Appearance

Quentin has silver hair, with two short strands of hair on each side, and a strand in front of his forehead that leans to the side. As a lakesprit, he has blue skin, and a blue gem on his forehead. Just like other lakesprits, he has two tails.

History

Quentin originally lived in a planet called Roccomenta. During his childhood, he and Emronia became best friends.

But, one day, during his early adulthood, he noticed that Emronia went missing. He didn't know why at first, but, the reason why Emronia went missing was because she was taken to the world of Duovantia.

Before Skygardening

After Emronia's adventure in Duovantia, Quentin was surprised to see Emronia come back as a mesprit. Emronia was brought back to Roccomenta while being accompained by Povynn. Povynn offered that Quentin could come to Duovantia with Emronia to see what Emronia did there. When Quentin came to Duovantia, he became a lakesprit.

Skygardening

Shortly after Quentin visited Duovantia, He went with Emronia and Povynn to Floralia. The three lakesprits joined the Council of the Seven Years, explored the mirror world, chased Neblino, and helped Sectonia defeat Dark Mind.

Abilities

He's good at dual-handed combat, as seen in Skygardening when he was given the Beetle Blades. He was also seen using wind magic to throw rocks at the King Golem.
