Emronia

From the Kespuzzuo Lore Compendium

Emronia is a character in the Kespuzzuo Fanwork Corpus. She is a mesprit, but she originally wasn't one. She was transported to Duovantia, where she was turned into a Mesprit, and met other Pokemon.

Physical Appearance

Emronia has silver hair, with two strands of hair on each side, and a short strand on top. As a mesprit, she has pink skin, and two pink gems over her eyes. Her tails have silver edges and silver gems.

History

Emronia originally was from a different planet called Roccomenta, where she lived with her friend Quentin.

PMD: Starscape Weaver

During her young adulthood, she was transported to Duovantia, which is a world inhabited by Pokemon, with this, she was turned into a mesprit. Povynn mentioned that Emronia might had become a Mesprit due to that species "fits her traits and lifestyle".

After being sent to that world as a mesprit, she woke up at a jungle, wondering what was going on, and reached a beach. At the beach, she met Debvye, who decided to accompany her. The next day, Debvye and Emronia received a map with a destination, and decided to travel there.

Before Skygardening

After her adventure in Duovantia, she came back to Roccomenta to meet Quentin. Povynn offered that Quentin could come to Duovantia with Emronia to see what Emronia did there. When Quentin came to Duovantia, he became a lakesprit.

Skygardening

Shortly after Quentin visited Duovantia, Emronia, Povynn and Quentin went to Floralia. The three lakesprits joined the Council of the Seven Years, explored the mirror world, chased Neblino, and helped Sectonia defeat Dark Mind.

Abilities

As a mesprit, Emronia is able to use psychokinesis. She's able to inhibit and grant emotions to herself and others. She also happens to be an expert at dual-handed combat.
