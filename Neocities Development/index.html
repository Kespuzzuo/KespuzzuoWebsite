Final stretch of the year

November 1, 2024

Somebody get Mariah Carey on the phone.

So it's November now. I originally planned to take a break from drawing and doing other fine arts during this month, but I thought it would be better to keep the momentum going.

With that new site with the butterfly that I set up, I feel like I got a motivation to keep going and going. I'm trying to get as many followers as I can, and rewarding them each time we reach a milestone.

By the way, here's the October music playlist.


But, yes. We're 2 months away from 2025. We're now at that time of the year where people are working really hard to meet deadlines and build their end-of-year projects. Oh! The drama is building up, the pace is picking up so fast!

As for me. Well, for me it'll be doing art orders and the remaining "events" of the year. I had no idea of what to do with the money I earned from drawing. Games? No, I already have a huge backlog across multiple platforms. Art? I draw art for myself. I want to look at my future, and tell myself that part of this money will be used to pay a portion of the downpayment for an eventual place of my own. Some of this money will also be used as an emergency fund.

Until Next Time,
